Features

Run your entire college efficiently

Schools that switch to Populi communicate better, get more people on board, and focus on teaching. All with less software at a much lower cost than before.

Recruit

Field inquiries, meet new leads, and introduce them to your college.

CRM

Get information, answer questions, and follow up with inquiries by text, email, or print. Dashboards, task lists, and simple reports keep your team focused on promising leads.

Inquiries

Embeddable forms funnel inquiries from your website or social media straight to Populi. Automatically assign your staff as new leads flow in.

Pipeline Reporting

See exactly where your leads are at and help them take the next steps. Study your pipeline to focus your recruitment and improve your processes.

Keep in touch

Connect with your leads however they prefer—SMS, email, print. Arrange and craft your interactions with communication plans.

Admit

Put your applications online and help students advance every step of the way.

Custom Online Applications

Design as many different applications as you need. Applicants can start on one device and continue on another. Move app data directly to a new student's profile upon acceptance.

Reporting

Monitor applicant progress, spot hangups and trouble spots, and process submissions. Later on, review the season for insights into your most effective reps, campaigns, and lead sources.

References

Request and receive applicant references from third-parties—securely, confidentially, and 100% online.

Questions & Answers

Applicants can submit questions directly from the application and your team can use in-app messaging to follow up.

Application Fees

Require upfront payment prior to starting or as the final step when it's time to to submit. Or waive the fee altogether.

Teach

Engage your students in class and online.

A complete LMS

Give your faculty all the tools they need—everything from enrollment to coursework to communication. Gated lessons, graded discussions, online tests, course evals, and a lot more, all integrated with your SIS.

Proctoring & plagiarism checker

Help students stay responsible with verified proctoring for online tests and plagiarism checks for all kinds of assignments.

Video conferencing

Stream live video lectures with Zoom and BigBlueButton and save recordings to the cloud for students to access at any time.

Online tests

Online test builder eliminates hours of busywork with automated grading for common questions.

Assignment submission

Students get secure electronic assignment submission. Faculty get an integrated interface for grading, rubrics, and feedback.

File management & video hosting

Upload files, video, and audio and use them in assignments, lessons, tests, and discussions. Content streaming is optimized for students' devices and locations.

Administer

Everything in its right place. Available to the right people. Anytime.

Student Information System

Unified student profile across entire platform, for the entire student life-cycle. Never worry about sharing data again.

Transcripts & Degree audit

You and your students see exacty what they have taken and along with what they need to complete to finish their degree.

IPEDS reporting

Create any kind of custom report you want. Standard reports like IPEDS? We built those in for you.

Online registration

Prerequisites, co-requisites, scheduling, and more. Powerful features make online registration a breeze.

Advising

Run what-if scenarios to see how taking different courses will impact degree completion. Customizeable flags identify struggling students.

Aid

Financial aid for your students when they need it without an expensive third party servicer.

Complete financial aid servicing

Process financial aid without using EdConnect or a bolt-on solution. Connect directly to DoED from a user-friendly interface.

Connect to SAIG Mailbox & adjust COD

Download documents from SAIG mailbox, sync awards to COD and resolve responses.

SAP Reporting

Track Satisfactory Academic Progress and flag students that are in danger of becoming ineligible.

Import ISIRs & process aid applications

Information from the FAFSA feeds directly into profiles and aid applications. Review error codes, aid counselings and MPNs.

Package aid offers & batch disbursements

Schedule disbursements using presets as well as built-in Pell tables. Disburse awards in batches and mark them released in COD.

Integrate with a third party

Still interested in using a third party? Connect with an outside system so you can pull in disbursed awards onto student accounts.

Account

Automated student billing that is clear, so that your students know exactly what is due.

Payment plans, recurring & mobile payments

Make it easy to pay by offering payment plans, payments from any device, or with opt-in recurring payments automatically drafted.

Enrollment Agreements

Design enrollment agreements that trigger based on student registration. Require signatures before students continue.

Exports for accounting software

Reconcile for a period and lock transactions. Run summary entries for accounting software or run exports.

Tuition and fee management

Tuition schedules trigger based on registraion. Fee rules trigger based on preset conditions. Invoice in bulk or individually.

Aging report

Keep tabs on receivables with a built in aging report that tracks invoices that are 30-60-90-120-121+ days overdue.

Advance

Build relationships with your supporters and work together to serve the next generation of students.

Donor CRM

Keep notes, schedule calls and follow up on to-dos on donor profiles. View annual giving and print individual and annual giving statements.

Online & mobile donations

Make it easy for your supporters to donate wherever they are, from any device. Custom donation pages can be embedded on any site.

Bulk Communications: SMS, Email & Print

Maintain regular contact with your supporters using all channels. Mail merge letters and print envelopes.

Campaign management

Set goals and create dontation pages for specific fund raising goals.

Alumni relationships

Continue the relationship even after students graduate. Alumni can update their contact info and provide info on current employment.
