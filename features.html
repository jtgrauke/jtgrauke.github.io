Features

Run your entire college

Populi handles everything from academics to donations, admissions to financial aid, people to reporting, and a lot more. It all works together—right out of the box—so your school can focus on its students.

Recruit

Field inquiries, meet new leads, and introduce them to your college.

CRM

Get information, answer questions, and follow up with inquiries by text, email, or print. Dashboards, task lists, and simple reports keep your team focused on promising leads.

Inquiries

Embeddable forms funnel inquiries from your website or social media straight to Populi. Automatically assign your staff as new leads flow in.

Pipeline Reporting

See exactly where your leads are at and help them take the next steps. Study your pipeline to zero in on future students and glean insights from past recruitment.

Keep in touch

Connect with your leads however they prefer—SMS, email, print. Arrange and craft your interactions with communication plans.

Admit

Put your applications online and help incoming students advance every step of the way.

Custom Online Applications

Design your school's applications to gather all you need to know from potential students. Upon acceptance, move app data directly to the new student's profile.

Reporting

Monitor applicant progress, spot hangups and trouble spots, and process submissions. Later on, review the season for insights into your most effective reps, campaigns, and lead sources.

References

Request and receive applicant references from thirdparties—securely, confidentially, and 100% online.

Questions & Answers

Applicants can submit questions directly from the application and your team can use in-app messaging to follow up.

Application Fees

Require upfront payment prior to starting or as the final step when it's time to to submit. Or waive the fee altogether.

Teach

Design courses and deliver them to your students, in class and online.

A complete LMS

Populi courses handle it all—everything from enrollment to coursework to communication. Gated lessons, graded discussions, online tests, course evals, and a lot more, all integrated with your SIS.

Proctoring & plagiarism checker

Help students stay responsible with verified proctoring for online tests and plagiarism checks for all kinds of assignments.

Video conferencing

Stream live video lectures with Zoom and BigBlueButton and save recordings to the cloud for students to access at any time.

Online tests

Build, administer, and grade tests and quizzes. Save hours of busywork with automated grading for common questions.

Assignment submission

Students get secure electronic assignment submission. Faculty get an integrated interface for grading, rubrics, and feedback.

File management & video hosting

Upload files, video, and audio and use them in assignments, lessons, tests, and discussions. Content streaming is optimized for students' devices and locations.

Administer

An SIS that works for all of higher education for the entire student life-cycle.

Student Information System

Populi's SIS works seamlessly with its LMS, billing, financial aid, and other functions. A single, unified profile keeps a complete record for every last one of your students.

Transcripts & Degree Audit

As students take courses, Populi builds transcripts automatically. You and your students see exactly what they've taken and what remains to complete their degrees.

Data however you need it

Standard reports that other systems make you work for. Custom reporting tools and an API for your own queries. Academics linked directly to financial aid and billing. Plus one-click IPEDS and other presets.

Online registration

Let Populi keep track of the pre-reqs, co-reqs, waitlists, and scheduling—and let your students enroll themselves in the right courses.

Advising

Run what-if scenarios to see how different courses will impact degree completion. Flag struggling students and get them back on track.

Aid

Package, disburse, and report on financial aid without an expensive third-party servicer.

Fully-integrated aid processing

Process financial aid without using EdConnect or a bolt-on solution. Connect directly to DoED from a user-friendly interface.

Connect to SAIG Mailbox & adjust COD

Download documents from your SAIG mailbox, sync awards to COD, and resolve responses.

SAP Reporting

Track Satisfactory Academic Progress and flag students that are in danger of losing aid eligibility.

Import ISIRs & process aid applications

Information from the FAFSA feeds directly into profiles and aid applications. Review error codes, aid counselings, and MPNs.

Package aid offers & batch disbursements

Schedule disbursements using preset timetables. Built-in Pell tables and aid origination fees. Disburse awards in batches and mark them released in COD.

Integrate with a third party

Still need to use a third party? The financial aid API lets you connect with servicers so you can pull disbursed awards into student accounts.

Account

A/R designed from the ground up to work with academics and financial aid. Billing your students and collecting payments has never been easier.

Tuition & Fees

Course registration automatically triggers charges on students accounts. Fee rules let you catch other situations. Populi automatically updates charges and refunds as students add and drop courses. Then you can invoice students in bulk or individually.

Payment options

Take online payments for tuition, fees, books, and fines. Offer students payment plans with options for recurring auto-draft payments. Send payment links to parents or other third-parties. Financial locks block access until payments are made.

Student access

Your students can see their complete financial situation right on their own profiles. Amount due, invoices, statements, enrollment agreements, you name it.

Exports for accounting software

Export your GL to reconcile with your main accounting solution. Afterwards, lock the period to prevent changes to your reconciled balance.

All the angles

See transactions, payments, unpaid invoices, unapplied deposits, and more for any period. Keep tabs on receivables with the built-in aging report. And if you to tailor reports for your school, the API lets you get at your financial data however you need it.

Advance

Build relationships with your supporters and work together to serve the next generation of students.

Donor CRM

Keep up with donors and run campaigns. Stay in touch with individuals, couples, and organizations via text, email, and print. Keep notes, schedule calls, and complete tasks on donor profiles. Review giving and print individual and year-end statements.

Take donations anywhere

Accept online donations or record the old-fashioned kind. Embed custom donation forms on any site. Let your donors set up recurring monthly gifts via credit card or electronic check.

Campaign management

Set goals and create donation pages for specific fundraising projects. Create appeals and track their effectiveness. Accept any currency and record gifts-in-kind.

Alumni relationships

Continue the relationship even after students graduate. Alumni can update their contact info and provide info on current employment.
